Pineapple Ginger Tea Recipe

Description

This is a simple recipe for a healthy, warming batch of ginger pineapple tea. It's a great way to make use of pineapple rinds. Serving size is about 4 cups. It takes about 10-30 minutes in total depending on how strong you want your tea to be.

Ingredients

  • Pot
  • Tea strainer
  • Mug
  • 4 cups of water
  • Rinds from one pineapple
  • A few inches of ginger, maybe a tablespoon or two
  • Honey if you want it sweetened

Steps

  1. Boil the water in the pot.
  2. While water is boiling, cut the pineapple rinds off of the pineapple and cut them into smmall strips, around 3 inches long and an inch wide.
  3. Mince the ginger or cut into small strips.
  4. Put the ginger and pineapple rinds in the water. You can put them in while the water is boiling or you can wait for the water to boil.
  5. Once water is fully boiling, turn down heat to maintain a simmer. Simmer covered or uncovered for at least 7 minutes, or as long as you prefer.
  6. Strain the tea into a large jar and pour individual servings as desired.
  7. Enjoy!
