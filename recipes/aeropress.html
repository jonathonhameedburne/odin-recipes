Chai Recipe

Description

This is a simple recipe for an amazing cup of chai. It takes about 7-10 minutes in total.

Ingredients

  • Tea strainer
  • Mug
  • 1 cup boiling hot water
  • 2 tablespoons of looseleaf black tea or 1 tea bag
  • Few splashes of milk
  • Four cardamom pods
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • Dash of ginger
  • Pinch of fennel seeds
  • 1 clove

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil the water.
  2. Step 2: While the water is boiling, scoop the black tea into the tea strainer or the tea bag into the mug.
  3. Step 3: Crush the cardamom pods and put them in the tea strainer along with the cinnamon, ginger, fennel seeds, and clove.
  4. Step 4: When water is boiled, add the water to the mug and let the tea steep for 5-8 minutes, depending on how strong you prefer.
  5. Step 5: Remove the tea strainer and add a splash or two of milk, depending on how milky you prefer.
  6. Step 6: Enjoy!
